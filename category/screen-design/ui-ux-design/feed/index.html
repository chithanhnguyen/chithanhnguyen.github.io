During the course Human-Computer Interaction (WS2018/19) at the University of Bremen, we (Vivian, Marlón, Jonghong and me) teamed up to create a prototype for a technology product that supports our daily activities. We came up with an environment-oriented App that lets the user collect reward points from every purchase with Bio, eco-friendly and Fair-trade products. The reward points can be redeemed for a wide selection of gift cards.
