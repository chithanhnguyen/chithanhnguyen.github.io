BACKGROUND

This photobook was created for Chuyến Xe Tuổi Thơ (rough translation: Trip to the Childhood, a charity club associated with the Vietnamese-German University for children in remote areas) as a visual report for the third trip to Sóc Trăng (Mekong Delta), Vietnam in January 2017.
